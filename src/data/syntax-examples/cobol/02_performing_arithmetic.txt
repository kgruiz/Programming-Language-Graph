       IDENTIFICATION DIVISION.
       PROGRAM-ID. ARITHMETICDEMO.
       DATA DIVISION.
       WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
       01 NUM1               PIC 9(4) VALUE 1234.
       01 NUM2               PIC 9(4) VALUE 5678.
       01 SUM-RESULT         PIC 9(5).
       01 PRODUCT-RESULT     PIC 9(8).
       01 DISPLAY-SUM        PIC ZZZZ9.
       01 DISPLAY-PRODUCT    PIC ZZZ,ZZZ,ZZ9.

       PROCEDURE DIVISION.
       CALCULATE-VALUES.
           COMPUTE SUM-RESULT = NUM1 + NUM2.
           MULTIPLY NUM1 BY NUM2 GIVING PRODUCT-RESULT.

           MOVE SUM-RESULT TO DISPLAY-SUM.
           MOVE PRODUCT-RESULT TO DISPLAY-PRODUCT.

           DISPLAY "Number 1: " NUM1.
           DISPLAY "Number 2: " NUM2.
           DISPLAY "Sum: " DISPLAY-SUM.
           DISPLAY "Product: " DISPLAY-PRODUCT.

           STOP RUN.
